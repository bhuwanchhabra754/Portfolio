Data transformation is the process of converting raw, unstructured data into a clean, organized, and insightful format suitable for analysis. My Work

Tapguard

Tapguard is a Smart Water Conservation System that prevents water wastage in hostels. It uses an Infrared (IR) sensor and GSM technology to monitor water flow, sending alerts via WhatsApp if flow exceeds eight minutes without motion detection. Developed over 17 weeks at Poornima University, Tapguard promotes sustainable water usage through real-time monitoring and efficient alerting, leveraging Microsoft Azure for management.
